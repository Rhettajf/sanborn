Typographic LEGEND

Typography of Sanborn Maps

Sanborn's fire insurance enterprise produced not only excellent and detailed urban maps, but they also maintained an elegant aesthetic in the headings and legends on the maps themselves, and in the title pages of the city volumes. The ornamental flair is diverse with no two style alike.
